About S.H.E.L.F.

S.H.E.L.F. stands for System Hub For Efficient Library Functions—a small web app for browsing a catalog, borrowing books, and keeping your reading activity in one place.

What you can do

Designed for learners and librarians practicing full-stack workflows.

  • Create an account and sign in with your email.
  • Browse the catalog after you are signed in.
  • Use the JSON APIs under /user and /admin for borrows, returns, and management (when signed in).

Tech stack

Flask, MySQL, and server-rendered pages with a calm “shelf” visual style.

  • Session-based authentication for HTML flows.
  • PyMySQL with per-request connections.
  • Schema scripts live in Database_Schema/.
